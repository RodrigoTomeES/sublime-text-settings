Sublime Grunt 1.1.0
===================

* Renamed main.py to grunt.py.
* Now, the Grunt Snippets package is included.
* Renamed SublimeGrunt.sublime-settings to Grunt.sublime-settings.
* Fixed if a Gruntfile list is canceled, and stop a `grunt` command.
* Relative path support. See https://github.com/tvooo/sublime-grunt/pull/97


Note
====

Please rename your SublimeGrunt.sublime-settings to Grunt.sublime-settings
and if your sublime-project use SublimeGrunt please rename to Grunt too.
